Shattered Pixel Dungeon

Warrior

The Warrior is a tank oriented hero.
He can tank damage really well due to his
starting item,the broken seal, which can be
attached to armor giving him bouns armor and
can also transfer upgrades and glyphs

Rogue

The Rogue is a melee burst hero. He can
easily one-shot enemies with his subclass
asssasin. He also has another subclass just
for kiting enemies as well. He starts with a
invisibility cloak, which is argueably the
best artifact in the game.

Mage

The Mage is a magic based hero. Since magic
damage is calculated differently from normal
damage and can never miss, he has a very powerful
early game. He starts with a staff of magic missile,
which has the some of the weakest base damage, not
including Prismatic light and Transfusion

Huntress

The Huntress is a ranged dps hero. She starts with
a spirit bow which has unlimited ammunition, making it
a powerful ranged weapon. Sadly, she has a poor early game
because of the spirit bow's poor scaling for damage even
she only needs a few win conditions.
